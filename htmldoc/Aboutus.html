About Us

An online movie ticketing system is a digital platform that allows customers to access the services of a business and buy tickets. This platform provides details such as what time a movie will be played, who the casts are ,price of a ticket and provies two payment modes that the user can choose from. A movie goer can login into the Cinema Ticket Reservation System,
search for movies and cinemas, view movie details, purchase/cancel tickets.
